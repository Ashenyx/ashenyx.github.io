ArtistCarrier
Change the carrier name within the status bar to your now-playing artist name!
Compatible with iOS 9(tested)
Note: It is possible for the carrier text to go out of sync if you change the tracks too quickly. To resolve this, simply pause the track and play it again and the carrier text will be synced.
